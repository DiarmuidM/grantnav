Data used in GrantNav

GrantNav contains primarily data published to the 360Giving standard. An up to date is on the 360Giving website. The table below lists the specific datasets used in GrantNav, and when they were retrieved.

We also use Code-Point Open to augment some of the location data, and Charity Commission data to look up charity names.

Reusing GrantNav data

GrantNav data can be used under the terms of the Creative Commons Attribution Sharealike license (CC-BY-SA). This means it can be reused for any purpose provided that you provide attribution, and release any derived works under the same license. You must attribute GrantNav, and also provide the information in the Copyright and Attribution section, as appropriate.
